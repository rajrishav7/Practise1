-About Us-

A Legacy of Trust

DCC Group is a proud owner of five companies under its belt. We are a leading name in the textile printing industry with an active and ever-growing distribution network. Mr. Narendra Dadia, the Chairman of DCC Group, has heralded unparalleled growth for the company over the years. From what was a humble beginning four decades back, today, we are well placed to meet the ever-changing demands of the global printing industry. Our founders have had excellent vision and foresight to help DCC Group carve a niche for itself. DCC Group today is the largest service provider in Asia for printing solutions for the screen, sublimation, and direct inkjet printing technologies. The challenges and changing industry standards excite us more than anything else. With innovation as our core focus, we remain dedicated to delivering cutting-edge solutions to the printing industry.
